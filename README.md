# From Nice Guy to Integrated Man

Personal development notes on the journey from **Nice Guy to Integrated Man** — drawn from *No More Mr. Nice Guy* (Dr. Robert Glover), attachment theory, and the *Dear Men* podcast (hosted by Melanie Curtin).

## View

**[Nice Guy to Integrated Man — A Personal Development Guide](https://eagleadams86.github.io/integration/Nice_Guy_to_Integrated_Man_Notes.html)**

## Contents

A single-page, dark-themed guide covering:

- **Core Philosophy** — the central reframes for how a man relates to himself and others
- **Understanding Nice Guy Syndrome** — what it is, the 3 archetypes, common traps, and the 6 fear responses
- **Shadow Work & Wholeness** — the inner integration work
- **The 5 Pillars of Growth** — vulnerability, embodiment, assertiveness, debunking myths, and fellowship
- **Dating & Attraction** — core principles, reading interest, the cold approach, common questions
- **Recovery & What's Next** — traits of recovered men and stepping into your power
- **Attachment Styles** — the four styles and their connection to integration work
- **Reading List** and an **Episode Index** of key *Dear Men* episodes
